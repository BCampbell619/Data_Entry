How to Close a Route

FOCUS:This guide will take you step by step through the process of releasing a route from a shipping dock door.

Select the menu option titled ‘Work With Routes – rtl’ and this ‘From’ and ‘To’ screen will appear:


When the ‘From’ and ‘To’ screen is reached you will need to enter your route number. The term ‘Route’ is synonymous with ‘Store,’ which can be found on your wave sheet. You can enter the route number in just the ‘From’ field, or it can be placed in both the ‘From’ and ‘To’ fields. After you have entered the route press ‘Enter’.

Once the desired route (i.e. the store number) is entered the next screen should display the route in an ‘Activated’ status as shown below:


The next step is to close the route. This is done by taking an option ‘5’ in the cursor field to the left of the route number and pressing ‘Enter’.

The ‘Activate Route’ option is the first option on this screen:


The next screen will have a three empty fields. Only two need information.


There are two fields that require information are the following:


  • Seal Nbr
  • Opt

Both fields require only a ‘1’ to be entered in both spots. (See image below)


Once these fields have been completed press ‘Enter’ and then ‘F16’.

This is a depiction of a completed route activiation:


Once the closure has been confirmed you should be taken back to the main route screen. Press ‘F5’ to refresh the screen and ensure the route shows a status of ‘Unassigned’.


Once this is acheived you have successfully closed the route. Be sure to mark your wave sheet of the changes made to the route, and realize that the wave associated with the route is now eligible to be invoiced.
