How to Invoice a Wave

FOCUS: This walkthrough will guide you through the necessary steps to properly invoice a wave/pickticket.

Where To Go?

Invoicing is done right from the ‘Work With Waves’ menu. As depicted below, option # 6 will take you straight to the pickticket for each wave you select.

Within the ‘Work With Waves’ menu select Option 6:


Pickticket

Once you drill down to the pickticket of the wave, there are additional options you can take against the pickticket.

The view after taking option 6:


Menu Options

The option that you want to concern yourself with is option # 51. This option is not readily seen at first on the pickticket display screen. To navigate to this menu option, or any other unseen menu options, just hold ‘SHIFT’ and press ‘F11’ to cycle through the options. Furthermore, all the menu options for any screen can be viewed in a list format by pressing ‘F4’ as well.

The option to invoice:



The view after taking option 51:


Completing The Process

Once option # 51 has been selected, all that is left to do is confirm the action by pressing function key ‘F16’. Once this is completed, the system will take you back to the pickticket display screen. Press ‘Enter’ or ‘F12’ to return to the main screen within the ‘Work With Waves’ menu.
This is how to invoice a wave.
